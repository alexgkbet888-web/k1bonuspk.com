Checklist

1. Time

Note whether the app closes immediately after tapping Allow or only after a short loading step.

2. Context

Record Android version, app version, and whether the issue started after update, reinstall, or cache clear.

3. Route

If the close happens after permission, review update, white-screen, and app-not-opening pages before reinstalling again.

Why does the app close right after permission is granted?

The crash can happen when the app state is corrupted, the update changed the permission flow, or Android accepted the request but the app still failed to load the next screen. The permission step is only the trigger, not always the root cause.

Evidence to keep before the next step

Capture the permission prompt, the Android version, whether the app closes immediately or after a loading screen, and whether this started after an update or reinstall.

FAQ

Why does K1 Game close after I grant permission?

The app may still be failing on load even though the permission request itself succeeded.

Should users deny the permission next time?

No. First record the crash pattern and app state so the issue can be isolated.

What facts matter most?

Android version, app version, exact permission prompt, and whether the crash started after an update matter most.
